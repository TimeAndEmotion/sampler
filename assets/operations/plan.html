Operations - Plan

As a conductor, has the composer given you a score which your orchestra can actually play?

How many of the audience will come back after the interval? Like Stravinsky’s Rites of Spring, will the audience be ready for it.

An annual planning tool is a good way to test the readiness of your team to produce – and to link to intelligence arising from market research or user acceptance testing.

Use the QfD at level 2 to produce Value Stream Analysis, identify silos, reuse and potential stream blockers.

Use the A3 templates to identify problems at team level. Prepare inititives and colutions to fit into team work flow. Report on the absorption phase.
