Opertations - Others

Putting a strategy into effect at the operational level may require effective interpersonal management in order to maximise the input of those who support transformative projects – and in order to minimise the risks presented by detractors.

Boisot’s division of your ‘audience’ into clans, fiefs etc. (see here for a prototype ready-to-use version) helps to work out who needs to know what – and what style of communication will best reach them.
