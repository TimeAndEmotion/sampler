Teams - Environment

The environment you and your team work in affects the quality of the work you do, from the space available on your desk and the dependability of your remote electronic connections, to less readily measurable factors such as perceived fairness of remuneration.

Maslow does not offer the best tool as there’s a tendency to jump the gun and head straight to ‘self-actualising’ (but someone still has to do the dirty work).

Herzberg offers a more practical check-list here, identifying the ‘hygiene factors’ (demotivators to eliminate) and motivating factors – working-through this is revealing.
