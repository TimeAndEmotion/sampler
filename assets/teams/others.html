Teams - Others

Moving on from your analysis of the wider environment, this step is about the influence which other people with your team, your organisation and your immediate networks can have upon your work – both positive and negative!

The tasks above will allow you to prioritise communications effectively – by identifying who really needs your attention, in what order.

In dealing with the day-to-day realities of this it often helps to see the problems from the interlocutor’s point of view, which this handful of excerpts from the ‘F-laws’ will help with.
